Lest We Forget

Remembering Radical San Francisco
October 2, 2014 to October 26, 2014

In the current state of profound, painful transition in San Francisco (income disparity, skyrocketing rents, evictions, displacement), it's more important than ever to remember the City’s radical history and hard-fought battles for social change. This wide-ranging series is not about quaint nostalgia for the good old days. This history is a living history, and how we live today is a direct result of the actions of the past.
